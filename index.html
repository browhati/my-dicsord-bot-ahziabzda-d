About Zen Bot

Zen Bot is packed with features designed to elevate your Discord experience. Whether you’re looking for advanced moderation, fun interaction tools, or ways to engage your community, Zen Bot has it all!

Features

  • Custom Commands - Create your own commands tailored to your server’s needs
  • Custom Events - Trigger events to engage users at key moments
  • Welcomer - Personalized welcome messages for new members
  • Emoji Manager - Add and manage custom emojis effortlessly
  • Ticket System - Provide organized support with a ticketing system
  • Logging - Keep track of server activities and changes
  • Reaction Roles - Assign roles with just a reaction click
  • Polls - Easily gather opinions with interactive polls
  • Timed Messages - Send scheduled reminders or announcements
  • Autoresponder - Automated replies to frequently asked questions
  • Embed Builder - Customize your announcements and messages
  • AI Chatbot (ChatGPT) - Intelligent responses to user queries
  • Image Generator - Create and send AI-generated images
  • Roblox Verification - Connect and verify Roblox accounts
  • YouTube Integration - Sync with YouTube for content updates
  • Leveling System - Reward member activity with ranks and roles
  • Starboard - Showcase your server’s best messages
  • Invite Tracker - Track invites and reward your most active promoters
  • Suggestions - Collect and review member feedback
  • QOTD - Daily questions to spark conversation
  • Giveaways - Organize giveaways to engage and reward members
  • Counting - A fun counting game channel
  • Birthdays - Celebrate user birthdays with reminders
  • Translation - Language translation for international communities
  • Weather - Real-time weather updates for any location
  • AutoMod - Automated moderation to keep your server safe
